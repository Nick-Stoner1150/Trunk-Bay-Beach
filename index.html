Trunk Bay Facts

  • Most Photographed Beach in St. John
  • Has an overlook at 3000 feet, perfect for pictures
  • 1 restaurant, 1 bar; both in walking distance
  • Rated one of the best beaches in the world!!!
  • 10 minutes from busy town, Cruz Bay

St John Island's most famous beach is also one of the most photographed beaches in the United States Virgin Islands. Over a quarter mile of beautiful white sand makes this the showpiece beach of the Naional Park Service.

Enjoy the self-guided Underwater Snorkeling Trail; with underwater information about coral formations and sea life. Facilities include a snack bar/grill, snorkel gear rentals, beach chair rentals, showers, restrooms, public telephones, and lifeguards.
